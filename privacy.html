Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2025

SpinShelf does not collect personal data without your consent. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

SpinShelf may use local storage on your device to save your vinyl collection data. If you choose to enable iCloud backup, your data may be securely stored in your private iCloud account.

If you contact us via the in-app support feature or email, we may receive your email address and the content of your message to assist you.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide and improve the SpinShelf experience. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

SpinShelf may integrate with services such as Firebase (for optional sync) and Discogs (for album info via barcode scanning). These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

You may choose not to use features like barcode scanning or cloud backup. You can also contact us to delete any email communication records.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@spinshelf.com.
